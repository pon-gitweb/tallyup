Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 November 2025

Hosti-STOCK ("the App") is a product of Stack Mosaic Ltd / Hosti Ltd ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information when you use the Hosti-STOCK mobile application and related services.

By using Hosti-STOCK you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

We collect information required to create and manage user accounts and venue access, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (hashed, never visible to us)
  • Venue membership(s)
  • Role (e.g., Owner, Manager, Staff)

Where this is stored: Firebase Authentication and Firestore.

1.2 Venue and Stock Data

We collect data necessary to operate a hospitality stock and ordering system, including:

  • Venue details
  • Departments and areas
  • Stock items, recipes, suppliers
  • Stock counts and history
  • Orders, invoices, deliveries
  • Suggested order data
  • Inventory cost and GP calculations

Where this is stored: Firestore and Cloud Storage (for related documents). This data belongs to the venue, not to individual staff.

1.3 Uploaded Files

You may choose to upload files such as:

  • PDF invoices
  • CSV files
  • Images/photos of invoices or stock items
  • Supplier catalog files

Where this is stored: Firebase Storage.

What we do: We parse invoice and catalog data to extract relevant information (e.g., line items, totals, pricing) to support reconciliation and ordering. Uploaded files remain private to your venue.

1.4 Device Information

We collect limited technical information automatically for security and performance, such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system and version
  • App version
  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Anonymous usage analytics

We do not collect contacts, SMS, or precise GPS location. We only access your files or camera when you explicitly choose to upload or capture something.

1.5 AI / Cloud Function Input Data

When you use AI-powered features (such as suggested orders, variance analysis, or Craft-It insights), we may temporarily process the relevant venue data via secure Cloud Functions.

We do not use this data to train public models. It is used only to generate responses for your venue.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and manage venue-level access and roles.
  • To process stock takes, orders, deliveries, and recipe calculations.
  • To analyse stock levels and suggest orders.
  • To read and reconcile invoices against orders and deliveries.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and user experience.
  • To provide technical and customer support.
  • To maintain security and detect misuse or suspicious activity.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Legal Basis (New Zealand Privacy Act 2020)

We collect and process personal information under the following grounds:

  • To provide the services requested by you or your venue.
  • To operate a legitimate business function in the hospitality industry.
  • To meet contractual, security, or compliance requirements.
  • With your consent, where applicable (for specific optional features).

4. Data Sharing

4.1 Service Providers

We use trusted third-party infrastructure providers, including:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Cloud Functions).
  • Cloud hosting providers (for infrastructure and logs).
  • AI/LLM providers (for optional AI features, accessed via server-side Cloud Functions).

These providers process data on our behalf and are not permitted to use your information for their own independent purposes.

4.2 Venue-Level Visibility

Owners and managers of your venue may access operational data generated by staff, including stock counts, orders, adjustments, recipes, and activity history. This is a core part of how Hosti-STOCK operates.

4.3 Legal and Safety Requirements

We may disclose information where required by:

  • Applicable laws or regulations.
  • Law enforcement or government authorities.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Stack Mosaic Ltd / Hosti Ltd, our users, or the public.

5. International Transfers

Data may be stored or processed in data centres located outside New Zealand (for example, in Australia, the United States, or other regions used by our infrastructure providers). We take reasonable steps to ensure that any international transfers comply with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020 or equivalent protections.

6. Data Security

We use a range of technical and organisational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Role-based access controls and Firebase Security Rules.
  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Secure storage and restricted administrative access.
  • Monitoring and logging of access where appropriate.
  • Hashing of passwords; we never see or store plain-text passwords.

No system can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we work to follow industry best practices to protect your data.

7. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as it is needed for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. In general:

  • Venue operational data remains while the venue account is active, unless deleted by the venue owner.
  • User accounts remain until deactivated or deleted.
  • Uploaded invoice files and related documents remain until removed by the venue or as part of routine data housekeeping.
  • Logs and temporary AI processing data are retained only as long as necessary for debugging, performance, or security monitoring.

8. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have rights to:

  • Request access to your personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of certain data, subject to venue and legal requirements.
  • Withdraw consent to optional features where applicable.
  • Complain to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

To exercise these rights, you can contact your venue administrator or email us directly using the contact details below.
